About Robots

How Robots can grow until now?

Robots evolved from simple automata to AI-powered systems, starting with industrial robots in the 1950s. Advances in AI, sensors, and mobility led to today’s humanoids, cobots, and autonomous vehicles, reshaping industries and daily life.

The Evolution of Robots

The robot revolution, powered by AI, is reshaping industries by enhancing efficiency and replacing human labor. While boosting productivity, it also raises ethical concerns like job displacement. Robots are becoming central to daily life and the future of work.

Ancient Automata

Automata were mechanical devices from ancient times designed to mimic human or animal actions, often for entertainment or ritual.

Industrial Revolution

In the 18th and 19th centuries, machines became more complex, enhancing industrial productivity and revolutionizing manufacturing.

Early Modern Robotics

The 20th century introduced robotics, with industrial robots like Unimate revolutionizing manufacturing and automating repetitive tasks.

Advanced Robotics Era

Today, robots equipped with AI are widely used in fields like medicine, military, and space exploration, becoming integral to modern life.

Future of Robotics

Future robots will be increasingly autonomous, working closely with humans in various industries and transforming our way of life

Path to Robot Revolution

With advancements in AI and automation technology, we are approaching a robot revolution that will globally transform how we work and live
